About

Hi there!

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Ferry Hasan, known as killflex or fleksipi. I'm 18 years old based in Surabaya, East Java, Indonesia. I have more than 4 years experience as a Motion Designer & almost 2 years as a 3D Designer.

It started from my hobby that loves to play games, watch cartoon animation, and also everything involved in design. I have the desire to make it by myself. In the end I started learning 2D and 3D design from various software such as Adobe After Effects, Photoshop, Cinema 4D, ,Blender, and so on.

Doing 3D & Motion isn't just hobby for me, but it also my biggest passion.
